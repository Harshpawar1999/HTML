Lasagna Recipe

Description:-

Pavbhaji is an Indian Dish.

With all the indian Spices and served with Pav.

Ingredients

  • Potato
  • Tomato
  • Cauliflower
  • Capsicum
  • Corriender
  • Mirch Powder
  • Pavbhaji Masala
  • Corriender Powder
  • Mustard Seeds
  • Butter
  • Cheese
  • Pav

Steps

  • Boil the Potatos, Cauliflower and Tomatoes.
  • Mash them all.
  • Take a Pan. Add some butter in it.Let it gets heated.
  • Once it gets heated add some Mustard Seeds in it.
  • Add potatoes Cauliflower tomatoes and chopped Capsicum in it.
  • Again Mash them all.
  • Add all the spices.
  • And Serve it with Pav and Cheese over the top.
  • And Our Pavbhaji is Ready to eat.
